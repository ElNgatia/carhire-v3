About Us

We are a car rental, vehicle leasing, and transport business situated in Nairobi, and our top priority is giving our clients the best possible service.

  • Professional Drivers: Our chauffeur driven prices include a guide around Kenya in addition to a driver. All of our drivers are skilled tour guides with in-depth knowledge of the nation's national parks, animals, birds, and other natural phenomena.
  • Transparent Prices: We have developed a straightforward and affordable tariff structure free of additional fees and expenditures.
  • Outstanding Service Delivery: We appreciate our customers, which is why our business was founded on a strong foundation to provide the best automobiles possible while also providing the greatest customer service possible with a personal touch.
